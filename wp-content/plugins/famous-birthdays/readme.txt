== Description ==
<strong>The Famous Birthday plugin</strong> for WordPress gives you on each day two famous birthdays. This is a fun way for you and your readers to se with which celebrites you share birthdays with!

This plugin varies celebs that still are alive with old stars and even explorers and such.


This plugin is provided by famousbirthdayplugin.com and uses the site to fetch data.

You may install this plugin on your website.


== Installation ==

 Upload The Famous Birthday Plugin to your website and activate.

    - Add the widget to your sidebar or any widget area or use the shortcode [famous_birthdays] on your post or page.

    - You're done.
